= Regenerate the Unified Manager client certificate
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
You can use the maintenance console to regenerate your Unified Manager client certificate. Your certificate has an extended validity period of five years.

.Steps

. Log in as the maintenance user.
. Select *Regenerate Client Certificate* and press *Enter*.
. If the system prompts for authentication, enter the required credentials.
. Select *Confirm Regeneration* and press *Enter*.
. After the certificate is regenerated, restart the Unified Manager service to apply the changes.
. To verify the updated certificate log in to ONTAP. View the client certificate status and the validity status.
